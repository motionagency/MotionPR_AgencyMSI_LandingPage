Two award winning Chicago communication mainstays, Motion PR and Agency MSI will be joining forces in February 2018. The union combines Agency MSI’s insight driven creative marketing with the power of Motion PR’s media chops to create an agency that will redefine what it means to be an audience-centric agency.

Marketing leaders consistently tout the need to shift from product-centric to audience-centric marketing.

By joining forces, brands will finally have an agency that can develop meaningful and impactful relationships, through the strategic development and deployment of authentic engagements with their audience. By leveraging both agency’s history of a people-first culture and balancing it with a tenacious need to keep brands moving and evolving, we’re cutting no corners in our mission to make people the focus of everything we do.

Motion PR is a public relations and marketing agency that believes that building meaningful relationships are key to moving brands forward in this new media landscape. Founded in Chicago in 2006 by Kim Eberl, Motion PR leverages digital marketing and public relations to help brands develop and launch robust communications strategies for brands in a variety of industries and have prided themselves in the deep Chicago media expertise.

Founded in 1961 as Marketing Support Inc. by David Weiner, Agency MSI is a marketing services agency focused on developing long-lasting client relationships and cutting-edge creative solutions to solve today’s business and marketing challenges. For the last six decades their family of curious thinkers, problem solvers, strategists, designers, writers and technicians all have been working together towards a common goal: create valuable and memorable experiences that get people to take action.
